About Me

Kate was born and raised in Denver, Colorado. She attended college in Fort Collins, CO where she received a degree in Hospitality Management. She dove straight in working in hotels straight from college, focusing on a career in Event Sales and Services. This career took Kate to live in many places, including; Maui, Calgary, and Vail. Life eventually took her back to her hometown of Denver.

Kate is a true mountain girl at heart and enjoys spending time outdoors; hiking, skiing, running, and camping. She loves traveling; specifically to the ocean and tried to visit somewhere new at least once a year. The true loves of Kate's life are her 6 year old son, Noah, and her loyal pup Luna. Kate loves to learn and master new skills. She is currently emrolled in a bootcamp to learn the ins and outs of code.
